Projects

graphr

graphr is a webapp that allows for better visualization of combinatorial graphs. It's a great way to quickly create graphs so you can get a better idea of how they are structured.


Using the MEAN stack and D3.js, graphr is powered by some fantastic technologies.

graphr is available for your enjoyment here.

TECH STUFF

MongoDB, ExpressJS, AngularJS, Node.js,
D3.js, HTML, SASS

SVBLET

SVBLET is a sleek solution for finding your next home. It seeks to find a balance between the wealth of options Kijiji provides and the elegant user interface of Airbnb.


SVBLET is a joint effort between myself and @KevinPan.

Check out SVBLET

TECH STUFF

Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, erb, SCSS, CoffeeScript

emotive

GradeTrackr

GradeTrackr helps you organize and track your grades to help you succeed in your academic career. You can efficiently search through your terms, courses, categories, and evaluations. GradeTrackr can also manage your tasks.

GradeTrackr is a joint effort between myself and three other Waterloo students; Adrian Gaw, Kevin Pan, and Michael Tu.

Get GradeTrackr.

TECH STUFF

Android (Java), SQLite
